Flow Overview

  • Authentication: Users can sign in or go through onboarding
  • Video Capture: Record or upload videos (max 60 seconds)
  • AI Analysis: Multi-modal analysis of voice, motion, and content
  • Feedback: Text, audio, and quantitative metrics
  • Actions: Save, share, or retry based on feedback
  • Progress: Track improvements over time
  • Offline: Record videos when offline, upload when connected

Feature Scope

  • P0 (Must Have): Core video capture, AI analysis, feedback generation
  • P1 (Should Have): Social features, advanced analytics
  • P2 (Nice to Have): Premium features, custom models
